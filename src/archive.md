# chain

we call this section "chain" because it is the immutable record of our discovery. in a world of fleeting digital noise and ephemeral content, the chain is our commitment to permanence. it is the ledger of our intellectual capital, where every verified block of research is stacked in descending order. this title represents the transition from active research to historical truth. once a block is moved here, it is locked. it becomes part of the shared, immutable history of the deepdive project.

what you should expect here is the deep storage of our validated episodes. these are the blocks that have already been mined—collections of 21 episodes that have passed through the mempool and the block template to reach their final state. this is the repository of our proof of work. but as you descend deeper into the ledger, the structure changes. beyond the episodic blocks, you find the most vital layer of the entire project: genesis.
