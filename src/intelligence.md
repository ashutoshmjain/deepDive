4. **noosemia**: superior linguistic performance triggers the human tendency to project "interiority" and intentionality onto machines.
5. **inference-native hardware**: computing is shifting from general-purpose processors to specialized language machines physically optimized for model execution.
6. **the energy-intelligence equivalence**: intelligence is a thermodynamic phase transition; the electron-to-token conversion is the final energy sink of the universe.
7. **the symbol grounding pivot**: referential grounding is sufficient for understanding; if vector relationships mirror physical reality, the model possesses functional knowledge.
8. **physical ai**: robotics is evolving into vision-language-action (vla) models, allowing machines to see, feel, and reason in unstructured environments.
9. **the prompt collapse**: technical mastery of arcane software syntax is being replaced by natural language intent; the tool is becoming the agent.
10. **the adaptive vanguard**: high-exposure workers possess the highest capacity to pivot, using ai as a force multiplier for human capital.
