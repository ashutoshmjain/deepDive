# DeepDive with Gemini

This repository contains the research and show notes for the "DeepDive with Gemini" podcast. The podcast explores a wide range of topics, from ancient philosophy and modern finance to cutting-edge technology and creative storytelling.

## Research for the Podcast

The research for the podcast is conducted using a variety of sources, including academic papers, books, articles, and online resources. The goal is to provide a deep and nuanced understanding of each topic, while also making it accessible to a broad audience.

The `promptsDeepDive` directory contains the raw research notes and prompts used to generate the podcast episodes. These notes are a mix of summaries, quotes, and questions that guide the conversation and ensure a thorough exploration of the topic.

The topics covered in the podcast are diverse and eclectic, reflecting a curiosity about the world and a desire to learn from a wide range of disciplines. Some of the topics explored so far include:

*   **Philosophy:** The Bhagavad Gita, Plato's Republic, and the nature of reality.
*   **Finance:** Bitcoin, the stock market, and strategies for financial freedom.
*   **Technology:** The future of computing, the role of AI, and the space race.
*   **Creative Writing:** The art of storytelling and the power of narrative.

We hope that by sharing our research, we can inspire others to explore these topics further and to engage in their own deep dives into the world of ideas.
