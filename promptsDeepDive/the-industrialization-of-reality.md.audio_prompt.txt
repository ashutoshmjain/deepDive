Main Episode with drum rolls:

So, let's dive into the core of the problem: we can no longer trust what we see. The "seeing is believing" era is over. So how do we verify truth? Not by trying to identify the fakes, but by verifying the real. This is where Nostr comes in. Nostr is a decentralized protocol that separates the signature from the file. Instead of embedding a fragile certificate in a video, a creator signs a separate "event" that attests to the video's authenticity. This signature is immutable and can be verified by anyone, even if the video has been re-uploaded a thousand times. It's a "Web of Trust" model, where verification is based on social consensus, not a central authority. In a world of infinite AI-generated noise, Nostr allows the signal to be heard.
