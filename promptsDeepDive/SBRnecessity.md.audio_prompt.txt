With federal debt exceeding $37 trillion and interest payments rivaling major social programs, the market's capacity to absorb new Treasury debt is strained. Traditional buyers are pulling back, making an alternative, appreciating asset on the national balance sheet crucial.

Second, the AI arms race is escalating dramatically. Achieving "war supremacy" in AI, extending to space-based compute and orbital data centers, demands an estimated $20+ trillion over the next decade. Private capital markets alone cannot meet this demand, as evidenced by Oracle's struggles to fund its AI expansion. The recent $1.25 trillion SpaceX-xAI merger and plans for a million-satellite orbital data center constellation underscore the urgency and scale of this endeavor, presenting a deterrent more potent than nuclear arsenals.
